About Me

Hi, I’m Thomas Manning (he/they).

Based out of Lansing, Michigan, I am currently pursuing my Masters of Science in User Experience from Michigan State University. Initially graduating from MSU with a BA in Information Sciences, I’ve always been interested in the world of UX and tried to take any lessons from my undergraduate work into my current field of Information Technology Infrastructure, such as updating and creating applications meant for inner-departmental use with UX philosophies in mind.

While versatile in my areas of interest, I am most passionate about evaluating and designing interfaces for services in the healthcare and event management industries. Maximizing the efficiency of the key user workflow is my number one priority, and this priority is heavily emphasized when looking at healthcare related interfaces.
